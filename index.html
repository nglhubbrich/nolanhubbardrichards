A little about me

Hey! It's a pleasure to make your virtual acquaintance! Allow me to introduce myself: My name is (in case you didn't catch my super subtle header) Nolan Hubbard-Richards. I work for Sarcan Recycling as an assistant supervisor at the Regina Vic East Depot, and have for the past ten years. With my deep passion for the environment, it has been a match made in heaven. Sarcan and I go together like Habanero peppers and strawberry jam (seriously. try it.)...

On the personal side, I live in Regina, Saskatchewan with my wife where I spend my spare time walking our 5 dogs (yes, you read that right: FIVE dogs. But 3 of them are chihuahuas, so they only count as 1) or 'arting' around. I play guitar, paint and draw, make model props from my favourite movies, coding and programming, or building projects with arduinos and raspberry pi. I think the term thrown around these days would be 'maker'. I like to make the things that I see in my imagination real. Be it a picture that I want to look at, a song I want to hear, or a homemade gadget to automate my home. If I create it in my mind, I want to create it in my hands. You can usually find me hunkered over my desk, working out the plans for my next project.

I like to consider myself a lifelong learner, as well. I love learning new things or diving into an area of study that I haven't tried before. Often, if in the process of creating the master plans for my next project I find that I am not sure how to do something, I will try as best I can to learn everything I can about that something.

But, that's enough of me gassing on about myself. Have a look around and I hope you like what you see!

- ngl.
