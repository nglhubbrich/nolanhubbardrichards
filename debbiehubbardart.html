If she's not working in the garden or tending to her Grand-Babies, Debbie can be found with either a paintbrush or pencil in her hand. Creating gorgeous Prairie landscapes, from snowy farmyards to the living skies that Saskatchewan is known for, as well as turning out life-like pencil sketches.

Due to some technicalities, the website is not currently living on the world wide web, please take these screenshots as a demo.

Project Details

My Mom, Debbie, has been painting and drawing and just generally crafty for as long as I've been around, and I'm sure longer. I have always encouraged her to show off her creations, and when I wanted to get my digital feet wet with web development, I thought of no better project than to build her a webpage to do just that.

Starting from scratch learning about different web frameworks and then seeing it all come together was awesome. Definitely a challenge, but incredibly fun at the same time. I got to play around with some very cool effects, such as a Light and Dark mode that gets triggered by the time of day (your eyes can thank me later) and an animated menu page. Definitely can't wait to do more!
