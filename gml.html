Project Details

The project was fairly simple, this YouTube creator was looking for Logo for his upcoming channel. Something catchy but simple enough that it would translate easily from the YouTube bubble-style avatar to website branding or even hats and t-shirts if the channel were to blow up. Looking for a funky twist on the classic circle-and-banner logo with a slim colour pallette. So, I loaded up Gravit Designer and went to town.

The words 'bright' and 'funky' were used a lot when in the discussions, so I summoned my inner '90's punk rocker; threw on some Blink-182 and grabbed some colours; switching them around a couple times to give some variety will make them pop no matter where he uses them. I'm hoping this channel takes off cause I think these would make some pretty sick hats.
